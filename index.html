Welcome

The Coronation Arms is a traditional, 18th Century Country Inn, family run, with five en-suite bedrooms.

Set in the heart of the picturesque Ribble Valley, the Inn has views overlooking the reservoir, a delightful beer garden to relax in to enjoy the summer and log fires in the winter.

The Coronation Arms is well known for its welcoming atmosphere and relaxed country pub dining. A popular choice for food, the child friendly 'Corrie' as it is known locally, offers excellent food, good wine and real cask ales. Homemade beefburger

The comprehensive Bar Menu and Daily Specials are available from 12 noon onwards every day. We also serve traditional roasts on Sundays.

A wide selection of the dishes from our Bar Menu are available as DeliReady meals in 400g and 800g trays for heating up at home.

For chocolate lovers, we suggest a visit to our in-house chocolate shop, DeliCoco, for our handmade Belgian chocolates.

With its warm and friendly character, the Coronation Arms is the ideal location for business, tourists, walkers, or anyone wanting a quiet, relaxing break.
